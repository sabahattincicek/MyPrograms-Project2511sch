    MyPrograms is a fast and user-friendly assistant where you can manage your schedules and easily organize academic materials in one place.

    Key Features:
    - Smart Timeline: Automatically calculates your lectures and exams.
    - Privacy-First: All data is stored locally on your device.
    - Study Materials: Attach PDFs and images directly to your courses.
    - Home Screen Widgets: Stay organized at a glance.
    - 100% Free and Ad-Free.
