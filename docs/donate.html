The Story of MyPrograms

I started developing MyPrograms because I needed a simple, fast, and privacy-respecting way to manage my own academic life. As a student, I was tired of apps filled with intrusive ads, expensive subscriptions, and complex cloud systems that track every move.

I decided to build something different: A 100% free, ad-free, and local-first academic planner. Every line of code in this project is written by me during my spare time between classes and exams.

Why Support?

Developing and maintaining an app as a solo developer takes a lot of time and effort. My goal is to keep MyPrograms free for every student out there, forever. Your support helps me cover the basic costs and motivates me to keep adding new features like cloud sync and better widgets.
