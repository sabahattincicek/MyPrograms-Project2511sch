MyPrograms

Built with ❤️ for students. MyPrograms was created to simplify academic management through a Local-First, privacy-respecting, and lightning-fast experience.

View on GitHub @sabahattincicek

The Vision

Developed by Sabahattin Çiçek, MyPrograms aims to bridge the gap between complex calendar apps and simple note-taking tools. By processing all your recurring schedules and study materials directly on your device, it ensures your focus stays on your grades, not on your data privacy.
